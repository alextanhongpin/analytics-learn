# List of aggregations that can be done

For numeric
- sum
- min
- max
- mean
- median
- mode
- percentile
- stddev

For categorical
- topk
- min freq
- max freq
- freq
- distribution


Time series
- rate of growth
- percentage >n%



General
- cardinality
- volume (e.g. how database tables grow at different rates for different tables)
